2024 Olympics

The Games of Unity and Excellence

Event Highlights

10m Air Pistol

Manu Bhaker won the first medal for India in the women's 10 m air pistol event. She won a bronze medal and became the first woman shooter to win an Olympic medal for India.

Shooting

In the mixed 10 m air pistol team event, Manu Bhaker partnered with Sarabjot Singh to win another bronze medal, becoming the first Indian since independence to win two medals in a single Olympics.

Men's 50 m Rifle Three Positions

Swapnil Kusale won another bronze medal in the men's 50 m rifle three positions event, marking India's seventh shooting medal across the Olympic Games.

Javelin Throw

Neeraj Chopra won a silver medal in the men's javelin throw event, becoming the fifth individual multiple medalist for India and the first to win a gold and silver combination.

Men's Hockey

Men's Hockey Team won the bronze medal for India making history.
